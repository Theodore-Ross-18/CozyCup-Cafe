About Us

Our Story


CozyCup Café began as a dream by Bermejo and Buala, two passionate coffee enthusiasts who believed in creating a space where people could connect over a cup of quality coffee. Our commitment to using fresh, ethically sourced ingredients has been the cornerstone of our journey.
